Weekly Reflection

Now that we are full steam ahead with our Capstone projects, I feel I really need to stay on top of my schedule and free time to complete the PWP, my individual responsibilities for Capstone and get ready for assessments. But I also know that my outside of class time should be used to build on the PHP and MySQL skills I've gained so far. With the rapid pace of the boot camp, I need to utilize that time better in order to feel confident enough to be productive in a job setting. I feel my strong points are my soft skills, I've always been comfortable around people, but I know that when it comes down to it I will need to show that I do know how to use these languages efficiently.

By working hard in class with my group responsibilities, I hope to focus my evenings on studying the 3 main languages I came here to learn: PHP, SQL and JavaScript. In my opinion, these are the things that will get my foot in the door, whether it be with a company or building a freelance site for a friend. Using these last 5 weeks to cement those skills to an intermediate level are my ultimate goal.
